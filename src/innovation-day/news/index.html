    Introducing PNSQC Innovation Day

    What began as a high school robotics demonstration has evolved into something bigger.

    On Sunday, October 11, the day before the PNSQC Conference begins, PNSQC will host its first Innovation Day. The event brings together students, educators, parents, technology professionals, and community members for a day of learning, exploration, and innovation.

    Innovation Day combines student projects, robotics demonstrations, and educational seminars into a shared experience. Students can showcase their ideas and accomplishments, while parents, educators, and community members gain practical insights into Artificial Intelligence, emerging technologies, and the changing world around us.

    Our goal is simple: create opportunities for students to explore future possibilities, connect with professionals, and engage with the broader community while helping everyone better understand the technologies shaping our future.

    Interested in participating? Student project registration is now open. We also welcome robotics teams, volunteers, mentors, and community partners.

    For more information contact outreach@pnsqc.com
