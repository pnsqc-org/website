Document Retention Policy

Standards and procedures for managing, retaining, and disposing of PNSQC organizational records.

PNSQC's document retention policy establishes guidelines for maintaining organizational records in compliance with legal requirements and best practices for non-profit organizations.

This policy ensures that important documents are preserved for appropriate periods while obsolete records are disposed of securely and systematically.

Key Document Categories

Corporate Records

Articles of incorporation, bylaws, board minutes, and other foundational documents that establish and govern the organization.

Financial Records

Tax returns, financial statements, audit reports, and accounting records with specified retention periods based on legal requirements.

Personnel Files

Volunteer agreements, contractor records, and other personnel-related documentation maintained according to employment law requirements.

Operational Documents

Conference materials, contracts, correspondence, and other records of day-to-day operations with appropriate retention schedules.
