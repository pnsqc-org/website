Call for Poster Papers — PNSQC 2026

Share your software quality idea in a visual, conversational format

Poster Papers are PNSQC's lower-pressure path for first-time speakers and practitioners who want to explore an idea with the community. Authors display a compact visual poster in a prominent networking area and talk casually with attendees about lessons, questions, and work in progress.

Bring a topic you are passionate about, field-test an early technical paper concept, or invite focused feedback from people who care deeply about software quality.

Bring your idea to the conference

Poster Paper authors share ideas in a relaxed atmosphere of peer engagement and review, making the format a strong fit for brainstorming, lessons learned, and practical problem solving.

Benefits of writing a poster paper:

Build confidence in a lower-pressure speaking format
Get focused peer feedback on an idea or work in progress
Meet quality practitioners through informal conversations
Receive 50% off conference admission if selected
