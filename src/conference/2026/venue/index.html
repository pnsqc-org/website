Venue

PNSQC 2026 will be held in Portland, Oregon. Below are the in-person venue, accommodations, and event details.

Attend In-Person

PNSQC 2026 will be hosted in the Willamette Ballroom at the University Place Hotel & Conference Center, located in inner southwest Portland, Oregon. The technical conference is on Tuesday and Wednesday, October 12-13, 2026; and Workshops follow on Wednesday, October 14.

  • Technical conference: Monday–Tuesday, October 12–13, 2026
  • Workshops: Wednesday, October 14, 2026
  • Onsite parking: $13 per day
  • Accessible by MAX and TriMet bus

Accommodations

For those coming to the conference in person, we’ve negotiated a discount at the University Place Hotel attached to the conference center.

  • Call the hotel directly at (503) 221-0140 and ask to book one of the PNSQC block of rooms.
  • The block of rooms for the conference is limited.
  • You may use the online hotel reservation process, but they can’t guarantee the discount rate that way.

Sunday Night Opening Reception

Join us on Sunday night before the conference starts, for food, drinks, and conversation. This is a fun chance to meet special guests, authors, and volunteers informally ahead of the conference. Anyone registered for PNSQC 2026 is invited to attend.
