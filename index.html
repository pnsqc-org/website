About PNSQC

Built by Practitioners,
for Practitioners

PNSQC is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit conference focused on real-world software quality. For over 30 years, we’ve brought together testers, developers, architects, and leaders to share knowledge and elevate the craft of quality engineering.

No vendor pitches. No hype. Just practitioners sharing what works—and what doesn’t. Everything from technical papers and hands-on workshops to keynote talks and community networking.
