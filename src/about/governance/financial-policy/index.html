Financial Policy

Financial management guidelines and fiscal responsibility standards for PNSQC operations.

As a 501(c)3 non-profit organization, PNSQC is committed to responsible financial management and fiscal accountability. Our financial policy establishes guidelines for managing the organization's resources in support of our educational mission.

These policies ensure transparency, proper oversight, and compliance with legal requirements for non-profit organizations.

Financial Management Principles

Budget Planning & Control

Annual budgets are developed with board oversight, tracking actual performance against planned expenditures throughout the fiscal year.

Approval Authority

Clear procedures define spending authority levels and require appropriate approvals for different types and amounts of expenditures.

Financial Controls

Internal controls protect organizational assets through segregation of duties, regular reconciliation, and oversight mechanisms.

Reporting & Transparency

Regular financial reports are provided to the board, with annual disclosures meeting legal requirements for non-profit organizations.

Reserve Management

Policies govern the establishment and use of financial reserves to ensure organizational sustainability and stability.
