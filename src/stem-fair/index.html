2026 STEM Fair

Sunday, October 11, 2026
•
1:00 PM – 4:00 PM
Portland, Oregon — University Place Hotel

A program sponsored by PNSQC for high school students in the Portland area to build skills by sharing projects in robotics, AI, cybersecurity, game development, and more. The STEM Fair will be held the Sunday before PNSQC 2026 begins.

Main Events

Robotics Team Competition

Build a robot and make it dance! Students program their robots to perform choreographed routines judged on creativity, technical execution, and entertainment value.

Our Vision

Why We Created This

We believe every student deserves a chance to explore technology, share what they’ve built, and discover where it can take them. The best way to teach is to invite students to an energetic and safe space, and let them freely explore. We do not just host a fair, we build the space.
