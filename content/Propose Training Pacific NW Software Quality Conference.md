# Propose Training Pacific NW Software Quality Conference

### Propose A Training Topic 

Are you passionate about Enterprise IT quality? Have a topic or hands-on experience you'd love to share? We're looking for training events to grow learning and engagement throughout the year—not just at the conference. Whether you're a first-time speaker, returning presenter, or industry expert, we welcome training proposals of all kinds. 

### **Lunch & Learn**

A casual, one-hour session—perfect for demos, case studies, or discussion-based learning during the lunch hour.  
**Duration:** ~1 hour  
**Ideal For:** Anyone with a focused topic or practical insight to share. Great for first-time presenters.  
**Format Ideas:** Live walk-throughs, short talks, Q&A, tool intros.

### **Special Event / Workshop**

An in-depth training experience, usually 2+ hours or even a half/full day. These sessions are typically led by experienced trainers or facilitators.  
**Duration:** 2 hours to full day  
**Ideal For:** Trainers, consultants, or experienced practitioners with a structured format.  
**Format Ideas:** Hands-on workshops, deep dives, bug bashes, interactive sessions, or themed standalone events.

### **Meetup Training Session**

A 60–90 minute session delivered during a PNSQC meetup—either virtual or in-person. These sessions are slightly more relaxed than workshops, but still impactful.  
**Duration:** ~1 hour  
**Ideal For:** Community members who want to share insights or test out a new talk in a welcoming environment.  
**Format Ideas:**  Live walk-throughs, presentations, case studies .

## Submit Your Proposal

After you submit a proposal, a member of the training committee will reach out to review your proposal with you.
