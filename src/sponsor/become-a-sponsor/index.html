Enabling Quality Through Community

PNSQC's mission is Enabling Quality Through Community. As a 501(c)(3) nonprofit, we exist not to pursue revenue, but to strengthen the software quality profession through learning, collaboration, and open knowledge exchange.

Sponsors who partner with PNSQC are not simply advertising — they are supporting a community-driven movement dedicated to elevating software quality across organizations, careers, and industries.

Why Sponsor PNSQC?

PNSQC is the only peer-reviewed software quality conference in the United States. Our proceedings and curated sessions create a relationship-first environment for meaningful engagement, rather than transactional exposure.

We prioritize high-signal conversations over expo-floor noise, giving sponsors direct access to practitioners, engineering leaders, and decision-makers who influence real-world quality and technology decisions.

What PNSQC Is Not

PNSQC is a relationship-first conference. We intentionally replace expo-floor noise and badge-swiping with deep technical engagement. If your goal is meaningful conversations with people who influence real technical and business decisions, PNSQC is unmatched.

Our attendees come to learn, contribute, and connect — not to collect giveaways.

Why This Audience Is Different

PNSQC attendees value depth over hype. They expect evidence, experience, and applicability — not marketing demos or trend-chasing talks. This creates:

  • ✓ Fewer but higher-quality conversations
  • ✓ Strong technical and leadership context
  • ✓ Long-term relationships rather than transactional leads
  • ✓ Alignment with mission-driven values

Who Attends PNSQC

Each year, PNSQC brings together more than 300 attendees from across the United States and internationally. Attendees include QA engineers, SDETs, developers, team leads, managers, directors, and executives from private industry, government, and education. More than half of attendees come from the Pacific Northwest and West Coast technology hubs, with representation from over 100 organizations.

What Our Community Is Focused On

Rather than static tracks, PNSQC organizes its program around evolving content pillars that reflect where software quality is headed — not where it has been.

  • ● AI-augmented software quality & testing
  • ● Quality engineering at scale
  • ● Leadership, decision-making, & quality culture
  • ● Ethics, trust, and responsibility in modern systems
  • ● Evidence-based practices and real-world case studies
