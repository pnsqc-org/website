Attendee Discounts

PNSQC is a nonprofit; we keep registration affordable on purpose. Here are the options:

To apply any discount, email events@pnsqc.org before registering. Include your name and which discount you are requesting. We will reply within 2-3 business days.

Group Rate

We offer a 15% discount for group registrations of 3 or more attendees.

Oregon Resident Rate

We offer a 10% discount for in-state residents of Oregon.


Please provide proof of current residency (ie. home address) when contacting us about this discount.

Student Pass

There is a special $99 rate for students for registration to all on-site events during the 2-day technical conference (workshops not included).


Please provide proof of current enrollment (ie. student ID) when contacting us about this discount.
