2026 Innovation Day

Sunday, October 11, 2026
•
1:00 PM – 5:30 PM
Portland, Oregon — University Place Hotel

The PNSQC Innovation Day brings students, educators, parents, and community members together to explore emerging technologies through student projects, robotics demonstrations, and educational seminars. By combining student innovation with community learning, the event creates an environment where attendees can interact with each other on topics such as artificial intelligence, robotics, automation, and other technologies shaping our future. Innovation Day will be held the Sunday October 11th, immediately preceding the PNSQC Software Quality conference.

Student Registration Seminar Registration

Student Presentations

Showcase ideas, research, and innovations in a professional conference-style environment. Students can present projects in STEM, artificial intelligence, software, cybersecurity, engineering, science, and related fields. Educators, industry professionals, and community leaders will be on-hand to provide feedback and encouragement.

Student Robotics

Demonstrate teamwork, engineering, and problem-solving through design, development and testing of their robots. Students will have the opportunity to showcase their robots, share their insights, and participate in demonstrations and competition activities with peers from across the region.

Education

Attend practical discussions about artificial intelligence, emerging technologies, and their impact on education, careers, and everyday life. Designed for educators, parents, professionals, and community members seeking to better understand a rapidly changing technology landscape.
