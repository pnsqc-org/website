Governance & Policies

PNSQC operates as a non-profit, 501(c)3 Oregon corporation established in 1982. We are an all-volunteer organization dedicated to bringing Quality Best Practices to anyone and everyone interested in learning.

Our Commitment: You don't have to know anyone special, be famous, or be part of an In Crowd to speak at PNSQC. Our merit-based selection process ensures everyone has an equal opportunity to contribute.
