Call for Abstracts - PNSQC 2026

Share your insights on software quality.

For over 40 years, the Pacific Northwest Software Quality Conference has brought together practitioners and researchers to exchange practical lessons, engineering techniques, and new ideas that advance software quality.

Quality in the Age of Autonomy - PNSQC's 2026 Theme

As software increasingly incorporates automation, AI, and autonomous systems, ensuring quality requires new approaches to reliability, validation, and trust.

We invite practitioners, engineers, researchers, and leaders to submit abstracts and share their experience with the community.

Conference Theme

Quality in the Age of Autonomy

Program Chair

Moss Drake

Software systems are becoming faster, more interconnected, and increasingly capable of acting with reduced human oversight.

Our 2026 conference examines how quality practices evolve across this spectrum—from primarily human-driven teams to highly automated and AI-enabled systems.

Regardless of tooling or maturity, quality remains grounded in intent, evidence, accountability, and learning.
