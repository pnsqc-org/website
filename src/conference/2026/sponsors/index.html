Why Sponsor PNSQC?

PNSQC is the only peer-reviewed software quality conference in the United States. Our attendees are QA engineers, SDETs, engineering managers, and directors -- practitioners who influence real tool and technology decisions every day.

Sponsors consistently report fewer but higher-quality conversations compared to large commercial conferences. This is a relationship-first community, not an expo floor.

We offer conference sponsorship at four levels -- Platinum, Gold, Silver, and Virtual -- as well as year-round community partnership options for organizations that want ongoing visibility with the software quality community.
