Privacy & Regulatory Compliance Policy

Our commitment to protecting personal information and maintaining compliance with privacy regulations.

PNSQC is committed to protecting the privacy and personal information of conference attendees, speakers, sponsors, volunteers, and all individuals who interact with our organization.

This policy outlines our practices for collecting, using, storing, and protecting personal data in compliance with applicable privacy laws and regulations.

Privacy Principles

Data Minimization

We collect only the personal information necessary to provide conference services, communications, and organizational operations.

Transparency

We clearly communicate what information we collect, how it will be used, and who will have access to it.

Security

Personal information is protected through appropriate technical and organizational security measures to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure.

Individual Rights

Individuals have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of their personal information in accordance with applicable laws.

Information We Collect

  • Registration and contact information (name, email, organization)
  • Submission and presentation materials
  • Payment and billing information (processed securely through third-party providers)
  • Communication preferences and interaction history
