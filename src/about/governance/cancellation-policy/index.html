Cancellation Policy

Understanding refunds, transfers, and cancellation procedures for PNSQC registrations.

PNSQC is a non-profit organization with limited financial resources. We must pay many fixed and non-refundable expenses, mostly well in advance of the conference.

Refund Timeline

60+ Days Before Conference

Full refunds are available for canceled registrations made 60 or more days before the conference start date.

Less Than 60 Days Before Conference

No portion of the registration fee will be returned for cancellations made less than 60 days prior to the conference.

However: Those canceling within 60 days retain access to the online proceedings and resources.

Registration Transfers (Recommended)

Preferred Option

We prefer registration transfers within your organization rather than refunds.

Substitute attendees can be arranged at any time without penalties.

Force Majeure & Event Changes

PNSQC disclaims responsibility for event modifications caused by public health officials, government agencies, or other authorities, as well as associated personal expenses from such changes.
