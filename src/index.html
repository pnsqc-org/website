Conference Theme

Quality in the Age of Autonomy

Program Chair

Moss Drake

Software systems are becoming faster, more interconnected, and increasingly capable of acting with reduced human oversight.

Our 2026 conference examines how quality practices evolve across this spectrum—from primarily human-driven teams to highly automated and AI-enabled systems.

Regardless of tooling or maturity, quality remains grounded in intent, evidence, accountability, and learning.
